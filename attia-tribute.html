Dr. Peter Attia Professional and Selected Personal Highlights

  • 1992-1996 - Bachelor of Science in Engineering and Applied Mathematics from Queen's University
  • 1997-2001 - Stanford University School of Medicine
  • 2001-2005 - Surgical Residence at Brigham and Women's Hospital in cluding chief resident
  • 2005 - Published "Hopkins General Surgery Review Manual" based on his surgical residency notes
  • 2003-2005 - Surgical Oncology Fellowship @ NIH/NCI
  • 2006-2008 - Engagement Manager at McKinsey & Company Corporate Risk practice
  • 2012-2015 - Co-Founder (with Gary Taubes) and President of Nutritional Science Initiative (NuSI) - Funders of objective, prospective nutritional trials
  • Husband, dad of 2, occasional blogger, bi-costal longevity/performance physician, potential book author
